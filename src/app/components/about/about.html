About Us

Our Story

PAYLOON was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to create clothing that prioritizes both comfort and individuality. We noticed a gap in the market for pieces that were comfortable enough for everyday wear but still had the unique design elements that make clothing special.

What started as a small collection of graphic tees has evolved into a full range of clothing staples - each designed with our signature attention to both comfort and distinctive aesthetics. Our founder, a former graphic designer, brings an artistic eye to each piece, ensuring that even our simplest items have thoughtful design elements.

Our Philosophy

Sustainable Design

We create timeless pieces that transcend seasons and trends.

Quality Materials

We use premium fabrics that feel good and last longer.

Unique Identity

We believe clothing should reflect individuality and personal style.

We believe that clothing is more than just fabric - it's a form of self-expression. Our goal is to provide you with pieces that feel authentic to who you are, while still being comfortable enough for everyday wear. We're not about fast fashion or fleeting trends; we're focused on creating thoughtful designs that you'll want to wear again and again.

Our Commitment

At PAYLOON, we're committed to responsible manufacturing practices. We work closely with our production partners to ensure fair working conditions, and we're continuously exploring more sustainable materials and processes. We know we're not perfect, but we're dedicated to improving our environmental footprint with each collection.

We also believe in giving back to our community. That's why a portion of every purchase goes to supporting arts education programs in underserved communities. We believe that creativity should be nurtured, and we're proud to contribute to programs that make that possible.
